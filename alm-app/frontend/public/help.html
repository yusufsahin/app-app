Help

User guide (HTML): After you sign in, open your organization, then use the Help (?) button in the header. It opens the full guide rendered from docs/USER_TUTORIAL_EN.md and docs/USER_TUTORIAL_TR.md (English / Türkçe).

Direct URL pattern: /{your-org-slug}/help/tutorial/en or .../tr.

Use the header search (Ctrl+K / ⌘K) to jump to pages and projects. Your team can set VITE_HELP_URL at build time to open an external site instead of the in-app guide.
